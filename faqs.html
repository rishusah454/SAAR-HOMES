❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties are listed on SAARHOMES?
We list rooms for rent, apartments, and houses for sale across the Kathmandu Valley — including Kathmandu, Lalitpur, and Bhaktapur.
Is there a fee for using SAARHOMES?
Browsing properties is free. We may charge a small service fee for property visits, deals, or legal documentation support depending on the service.
Can I list my own property on SAARHOMES?
Yes! Property owners can list their rooms or houses by contacting us directly or submitting through the "List Your Property" section on our website.
How often are listings updated?
We update our listings regularly to ensure all available properties are current. Sold or rented properties are marked or removed promptly.
Do you provide site visits?
Yes, we arrange site visits to help you explore the property in person. You can book a visit through the contact form or call us directly.
